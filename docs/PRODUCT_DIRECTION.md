# Product Direction

## Decision

This repo is moving from a one-course study demo into a curated premium study-course platform.

The product is not an upload platform. Students and instructors do not upload their own material. The platform owner publishes curated courses, and students study them through structured lessons, quizzes, flashcards, practice problems, formula banks, progress tracking, and AI-assisted study flows.

## Current State

The current app is a useful proof of concept for one course. It has useful study features, but its identity and storage are hardcoded around one course.

## Target Product

The app should show a catalog of curated courses. Each course should contain modules, lessons, quizzes, flashcards, practice problems, formula/reference pages, and exam preparation tools.

## Product Principles

1. Curated quality over random uploads.
2. Course-agnostic platform logic.
3. Reusable learning engines.
4. Student-first progress and weak-topic guidance.
5. Architecture that can later support paid access and premium courses.

## MVP Scope

- Course catalog
- Course detail page
- Module and lesson structure
- Generic lesson viewer
- Generic flashcards
- Generic quiz engine
- Generic practice problem viewer
- Generic formula bank
- Course-scoped progress tracking
- Existing one-course demo migrated as the first course pack

## Non-Goals for Now

- No student upload system
- No instructor upload system
- No public marketplace
- No heavy LMS features before the study experience is excellent

## North Star

Turn curated course content into an interactive study experience that helps students understand, memorize, practice, and prepare for exams.
